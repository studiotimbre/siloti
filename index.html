music ambassador • composer • conductor • pianist

Alexander Ilyich Siloti led musicians to inspire and sustain the Russian people through tumultuous and tragic wars and political transition. Siloti’s crafting of musical moments in St. Petersburg Siloti Concerts (1903-1917) and Duma Hall People’s Free Concerts (1915-1917) fostered the formation of music for the people at the pinnacle of world musical art. Siloti’s unbridled musical clarity, moral character, pianistic expressive power, and heart for people fused connections across musical artists and audiences.
